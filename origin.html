Coffee Origin

100% Coffea Arabica · Ethiopian Highlands

The Birthplace of Coffee

Grown at 1,900–2,200 meters

Our coffee is cultivated by small-scale farmers in traditional garden farms across Ethiopia's renowned coffee regions. The cool highland climate, natural shade trees, fertile volcanic soil, and balanced seasonal rainfall create perfect conditions for slow-ripening cherries.

This careful, natural process allows the beans to develop complex flavors that are uniquely Ethiopian — bright, fruity, and incomparably smooth.

Roast Profile

Medium-Dark Roast

All roasted coffees from Bean to Bag are roasted to a Medium-Dark roast level. This profile provides balanced acidity, full body, rich chocolate and cocoa notes, and a smooth finish.

Perfect for: Jebena brewing · Espresso · Macchiato · French press · Milk-based drinks

Supporting Smallholder Farmers

We work directly with traditional garden farm families, ensuring fair prices and long-term relationships that sustain Ethiopia's coffee heritage for generations to come.
