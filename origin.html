The Ethiopian Coffee Journey

The Jebena Ritual

The coffee ceremony is the heart of Ethiopian hospitality. Fresh beans are roasted in a pan over charcoal, filling the room with intoxicating aroma. The roasted beans are then ground using a mortar and pestle, then brewed in a clay pot called a Jebena. This timeless ritual symbolizes friendship, respect, and the soul of the nation.

☕ Three Rounds🔥 Open-fire roasting

Master Roasting

Small-batch roasting is an art form. We use precision roasting to unlock notes of wild berries, jasmine, dark chocolate, and a winey acidity. Our medium-dark profile delivers a smooth, balanced cup that works beautifully for espresso, filter, or Jebena.
