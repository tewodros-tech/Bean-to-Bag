The Birthplace of Coffea Arabica

100% Arabica grown in traditional garden farms at 1,900–2,200 meters above sea level. Discover the terroir that shapes every Bean to Bag cup.

The Land

High Altitude · Slow Ripening

Our coffee is cultivated by small-scale farmers in traditional garden systems under natural shade. The cool climate, volcanic soil, and balanced rainfall allow cherries to mature slowly — developing complex sugars and dense flavor compounds.

1,900 – 2,200 meters above sea level — one of the highest cultivation zones, producing refined brightness and floral aroma.

Direct Relationships

From Smallholder to Your Bag

We work directly with farmer cooperatives in Sidama, Yirgacheffe, and Guji zones. By paying premium prices and maintaining traceability, we ensure farmers receive fair compensation while you receive exceptional, single-origin coffee.

Tradition & Heritage

More than a beverage — a ritual of hospitality, connection, and blessing. Our medium-dark roast is the perfect bridge between ancient Jebena preparation and modern espresso culture.
