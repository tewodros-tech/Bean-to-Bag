Birthplace of Coffee

In the misty forests of Ethiopia’s highlands, at 1,900-2,200 meters, our partner farmers cultivate heirloom varieties using traditional methods. Volcanic soils and ancient shade trees give birth to cherries that burst with jasmine, bergamot and wild berry notes — a taste profile impossible to replicate elsewhere.

Bean to Bag works directly with washing stations and women-led cooperatives, ensuring traceability and ethical premiums. This is coffee that tells a story of land, legacy, and love.

Selective Harvest

Only deep-red, perfectly ripe cherries are handpicked by skilled farmers. This meticulous selection unlocks bright acidity and complex sweetness. Each cherry is sorted within hours to preserve purity — a mark of true Ethiopian specialty grade.

Learn more →

Traditional Processing

Washed and natural sundried on raised beds, our methods highlight clarity and fruit-forward elegance. Slow fermentation, constant turning, and Ethiopian sun develop honeyed sweetness and floral perfume.

Our process →

Small-Batch Roasting

Medium-dark roast profile: balanced acidity, full body, notes of dark chocolate, toasted almond and subtle spice. Roasted weekly to ensure peak freshness — each bag stamped with roast date and batch ID.
