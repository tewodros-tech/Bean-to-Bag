☕ Our Story

Crafted with Passion from Ethiopian Highlands

Bean to Bag is dedicated to delivering high-quality Ethiopian coffee from origin to cup. Our coffee is grown by smallholder farmers (many of them women) in traditional garden farms across Ethiopia's highlands at 1,900–2,200 meters above sea level. These are the same altitudes where coffee was first discovered over 1,000 years ago.

Every step — from sourcing green beans to roasting and packaging — is carefully handled to ensure freshness and quality. We work directly with farmer cooperatives, bypassing middlemen to ensure fair prices for the hardworking families who produce our exceptional beans. The name "Bean to Bag" represents the full coffee journey: Coffee Bean → Roasting → Packaging → Customer.

Our mission is to preserve Ethiopian coffee heritage while providing a sustainable livelihood for the farmers who make it all possible. When you drink Bean to Bag coffee, you're not just enjoying a great cup — you're supporting Ethiopian communities and sustaining a centuries-old tradition.

Coffee Origin

Where Our Coffee Grows

Our coffee is cultivated by small-scale farmers (many women-led cooperatives) in traditional garden farms at 1,900–2,200 meters above sea level. The cool highland climate, natural shade trees, fertile soil, and balanced seasonal rainfall allow coffee cherries to ripen slowly, developing refined and complex flavors.

The regions of Yirgacheffe, Sidamo, Harrar, and Guji are renowned worldwide for producing some of the finest Arabica beans. Each region has its unique microclimate that imparts distinct flavor characteristics to the coffee.

Floral Aroma

Elegant and delicate floral notes with jasmine and bergamot undertones

Bright Acidity

Lively and vibrant acidity profile, similar to fine wine

Citrus & Fruit Notes

Ripe citrus, blueberry, and dark cherry undertones

Smooth Clean Finish

Clean, refined aftertaste with no bitterness

Roast Profile

Medium-Dark Roast Perfection

All roasted coffees from Bean to Bag are roasted to a balanced Medium-Dark roast level. This provides balanced acidity, full body, rich chocolate and cocoa notes, and a smooth finish that works perfectly with all brewing methods — from traditional jebena to modern espresso machines.

Our master roasters carefully develop each batch to bring out the unique terroir of Ethiopian highland coffee while maintaining the perfect balance between acidity, body, and sweetness. We roast in small batches of 10-15kg to ensure consistency and freshness.

Our Process

From Farm to Your Cup

Every step in our process is carefully crafted to deliver perfection, ensuring that the unique flavors of Ethiopian highland coffee are preserved from cherry to cup.

01

Sourcing

Direct partnerships with Ethiopian highland farmers for the finest Arabica beans at 1,900–2,200m altitude. We work with 15+ farmer cooperatives across Yirgacheffe, Sidamo, and Guji regions, ensuring fair prices and sustainable farming practices.

02

Selection

Hand-picked and carefully sorted to ensure only the best ripe cherries are used for our premium blends. Our quality control team inspects every batch, rejecting any beans that don't meet our strict standards for size, density, and moisture content.

03

Roasting

Small-batch roasted (10-15kg) to a balanced medium-dark profile for optimal flavor and consistency. Our master roasters with 15+ years experience carefully develop each batch to bring out the unique terroir and flavor notes of each micro-region.

04

Packaging

Premium stand-up bags with one-way degassing valve, zip-lock resealable top, and matte finish. Freshly sealed within 24 hours of roasting and delivered to your door. Each bag includes roast date, batch number, and brewing recommendations.
