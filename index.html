Our Heritage

Highlands of
Ethiopia

Our coffee is cultivated by small-scale farmers in traditional garden farms at 1,900–2,200 meters above sea level. The cool highland climate, natural shade trees, and fertile soil allow cherries to ripen slowly, developing refined and complex flavors unique to Ethiopian Arabica.
