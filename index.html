The Bean to Bag Promise

Built for the full coffee journey.

Bean to Bag is not only a name. It is the standard: carefully selected cherries, responsible relationships, controlled roasting, and professional packaging that protects the life inside every bean.

"A bag should feel like the origin is still breathing when you open it."
01 / Sourcing

Highland smallholders

Coffee grows slowly under shade, volcanic soil, and cool mountain air, giving the cup its clean acidity and layered aromatics.

02 / Roasting

Medium-dark discipline

Roasting is developed for chocolate body and caramel depth without burying the floral character Ethiopia is known for.

03 / Packing

Freshness with proof

Every bag can carry roast date, batch identity, grind choice, and a one-way valve to protect aroma after degassing.

Origin With Memory

From cool highlands to a cup with clarity.

Our Ethiopian lots are selected for altitude, harvest care, and expressive sensory profile. The result is coffee that feels vivid but grounded: floral on the nose, sweet through the middle, and smooth at the finish.

1,900–2,200 m

Slow cherry maturation encourages sweetness, elegant acidity, and a denser green bean structure that responds beautifully to careful roasting.

100% Arabica

Selected for balance and aromatic lift, then roasted to preserve origin character while adding cocoa, toasted sugar, and rounded body.

Traceable lots

Batch details help customers connect the finished bag to region, process, roast date, and the people behind the harvest.

From Cherry To Cup

A process you can taste.

The strongest design choice in coffee is restraint. Each step protects what the farm created instead of covering it up later.

Selective harvest

Only ripe cherries are picked, reducing harshness and improving sweetness in the final cup.

Washed clarity

Careful fermentation and washing highlight floral aroma, citrus brightness, and a clean finish.

Raised bed drying

Slow drying supports stable moisture and lets the green coffee arrive ready for precise roasting.

Small-batch roast

Curves are tuned for balance: developed body, sweet aromatics, and no flat burnt finish.

Sealed fresh

Valve bags, batch information, and grind options keep the coffee expressive after roasting.
