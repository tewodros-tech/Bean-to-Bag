THE BEAN TO BAG PROMISE

Built for the full coffee journey.

Bean to Bag is not only a name. It is the standard: carefully selected cherries, responsible relationships, controlled roasting, and professional packaging that protects the life inside every bean.

"A bag should feel like the origin is still breathing when you open it."

01 / SOURCING

Highland smallholders

Coffee grows slowly under shade, volcanic soil, and cool mountain air, giving the cup its clean acidity and layered aromatics.

02 / ROASTING

Medium-dark discipline

Roasting is developed for chocolate body and caramel depth without burying the floral character Ethiopia is known for.

03 / PACKING

Freshness with proof

Every bag carries roast date, batch identity, grind choice, and a one-way valve to protect aroma after degassing.
