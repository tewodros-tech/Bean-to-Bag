Our Heritage

100% Coffea Arabica from Ethiopia's Highlands

Our coffee is cultivated by small-scale farmers in traditional garden farms at 1,900–2,200 meters above sea level. The cool highland climate, natural shade trees, fertile soil, and balanced seasonal rainfall allow the coffee cherries to ripen slowly, developing refined and complex flavors.

Roast Profile

Medium-Dark Roast

All roasted coffees from Bean to Bag are roasted to a Medium-Dark roast level. This provides balanced acidity, full body, rich chocolate and cocoa notes, and a smooth finish. Perfect for Jebena brewing, espresso, macchiato, French press, and milk-based drinks.
