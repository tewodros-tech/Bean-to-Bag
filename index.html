Brewing Guide

Brew the Perfect Cup

Our coffee is versatile and works well with all brewing methods. Here are our top recommended ways to enjoy Bean to Bag coffee:

Jebena (Traditional Ethiopian)

Use coarse grind, boil with water, and let steep for 4-5 minutes for authentic flavor.

Espresso

Use fine grind, 18-20g dose, 25-30 second pull for a rich, concentrated shot.

French Press

Use coarse grind, 1:15 coffee-to-water ratio, steep for 4 minutes, press slowly.

Pour Over (V60)

Use medium-fine grind, 1:16 ratio, pour in circular motion for a clean, bright cup.
