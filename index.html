Our Journey

From Farm to Cup

Experience the craftsmanship behind every bag of Bean to Bag coffee.

Highland Farms

Our coffee is cultivated by small-scale farmers in traditional garden farms at 1,900–2,200 meters above sea level. The cool highland climate, natural shade trees, and fertile volcanic soil allow cherries to ripen slowly, developing refined and complex flavors.

Discover Origin →

Selective Harvest

Only perfectly ripe cherries are hand-picked by our partner farmers. This meticulous selection process ensures that every bean meets our quality standards, resulting in a cup with bright acidity, floral aroma, and exceptional sweetness.

Learn More →

Traditional Processing

Using both washed and natural processing methods, we preserve the unique characteristics of each lot. Our careful fermentation and drying on raised beds develop the coffee's signature clarity and fruit-forward notes.

View Products →

Small-Batch Roasting

Every batch is roasted to a precise medium-dark profile, balancing acidity and body while developing rich chocolate and cocoa notes. Our small-batch approach ensures freshness and allows us to highlight each coffee's unique character.

Our Process →

Premium Packaging

Our stand-up bags feature one-way degassing valves and zip-lock resealable tops to preserve freshness. Each bag includes roast date, batch number, and brewing recommendations — because you deserve to know exactly what you're brewing.

Roast Profile

Medium-Dark Roast

All roasted coffees from Bean to Bag are roasted to a Medium-Dark roast level. This provides balanced acidity, full body, rich chocolate and cocoa notes, and a smooth finish. Perfect for Jebena brewing, espresso, macchiato, French press, and milk-based drinks.
