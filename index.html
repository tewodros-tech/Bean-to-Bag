The Ethiopian Coffee Chronicle

Witness the soulful journey — from red cherry to aromatic cup, captured in 8 cinematic moments.

10 · The Harvest Dawn

At sunrise, skilled pickers traverse misty highlands, hand-selecting only the ripest crimson cherries. This labor of love ensures every bean reaches peak sweetness before beginning its transformation.

📍 Sidama Region🌿 Hand sorting

11 · The Washing Stream

Cherries are floated and graded in pristine spring water. Dense, premium beans sink while defects are removed — an ancient method preserving clarity and brightness.

💧 Spring-fed♻️ Eco-wet mill

12 · African Beds

Parchment coffee rests on raised nets under the sun, turned every hour for even drying. This slow, 18-day process unlocks floral and honey-like nuances.

☀️ Sun-dried🛏️ Raised Beds

13 · Jebena Ritual

The traditional Ethiopian coffee ceremony — roasting beans over open flame, grinding with mortar, and brewing in a clay Jebena. A symbol of community and respect.

🔥 Fresh roast🍃 Incense & popcorns

14 · Master Roaster

Small-batch artisanal roasting with precision curves: each bean reaches first crack with unwavering control, producing a symphony of chocolate, caramel, and berry esters.

🎚️ Loring roaster📊 Real-time profiling

15 · Aromatic Grind

The moment of grinding releases volatile oils — jasmine, bergamot, wild berries. It’s the sensory bridge between bean and cup.

⚙️ Burr grinders🌺 Fragrant bloom

16 · The Pour

Precision pour-over technique extracts layers of sweetness, clarity, and silky body. Each cup tells a story of altitude, soil, and craftsmanship.

💧 93°C water🧪 TDS: 1.3%

17 · Breaking Bread

Shared coffee is the cornerstone of Ethiopian hospitality. From family gatherings to business meetings, coffee is sacred. This frame honors connection.

🤝 Unity🏔️ Highland tradition

19 · Bean to Bag

Our final artisan touch: nitrogen-flushed bags protect the beans until you open them. From farm to your grinder, traceable, fresh, uncompromising.
