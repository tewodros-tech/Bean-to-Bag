The Bean to Bag Promise

Honoring Ethiopia’s coffee soul

🌸 Women in Coffee: 60% of our partner farmers are women. We ensure fair wages, training, and long-term contracts.

Bunna Tetu • Culture

The Jebena ceremony

In Ethiopia, coffee is a sacred ritual of connection. Women roast green beans over hot coals, grind by hand, and brew in a clay jebena. The aroma fills the room — three rounds of coffee (Abol, Tona, Baraka) bless friendships. Bean to Bag honors this heritage: every bag is crafted for ceremony and daily joy.

Women-led farming groups — we partner with female coffee growers, empowering them through fair prices and long-term contracts.
