Our Legacy

The Essence of Specialty Coffee

Ethiopia is the birthplace of Arabica coffee. At Bean to Bag, we preserve this ancient heritage through rigorous quality selection and artisan roasting techniques.

Sourced from Historic Origins

Our beans are selected directly from sustainable farms in famous growing regions like Yirgacheffe, Sidamo, and Guji. We prioritize fair trade principles to uplift smallholder farmers who treat coffee cultivation as a generational art form.

Signature Master Roast

The Medium-Dark Philosophy

Instead of masking the profile beneath heavy smoke, our deliberate medium-dark roast approach deepens the natural structural body of Ethiopian coffee. This allows standard caramel and chocolate notes to comfortably settle without dissolving the delicate top notes of floral jasmine and bright citrus acidity.
