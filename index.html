Crafted with Passion from Ethiopian Highlands

Bean to Bag is dedicated to delivering high-quality Ethiopian coffee from origin to cup. Our coffee is grown by smallholder farmers in traditional garden farms across Ethiopia's highlands.

Every step — from sourcing green beans to roasting and packaging — is carefully handled to ensure freshness and quality.
