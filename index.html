The Bean to Bag Promise

Freshly Roasted Ethiopian Specialty Coffee

Bean to Bag represents the complete coffee journey — from carefully sourced Ethiopian green beans to freshly roasted and professionally packaged coffee delivered to customers. We work directly with small-scale farmers who cultivate 100% Coffea Arabica using traditional methods passed down through generations.

The cool highland climate, natural shade trees, fertile volcanic soil, and balanced seasonal rainfall allow the coffee cherries to ripen slowly, developing refined floral aromas, bright acidity, and smooth clean finish.
