LEGACY

Birthplace of Arabica

Ethiopia is the cradle of coffee, where wild arabica still grows under the shade of ancient trees. Our beans come from family-owned garden farms between 1,900–2,200 meters — volcanic soil, misty mornings, and generations of wisdom. Each cherry is handpicked at peak ripeness, preserving the wild floral aroma and bright complexity that define Ethiopian coffee.

We honor this tradition by building direct relationships with smallholder cooperatives, ensuring fair prices and traceable excellence. From the forests of Kaffa to your brewing device, Bean to Bag tells the true story of origin.
