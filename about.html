Our Story

Bean to Bag Coffee

Coffee roasting, packaging, and distribution business based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. We bring the finest Ethiopian Arabica from highland farms to your cup.

From Origin to Cup — With Intention

Bean to Bag is dedicated to delivering high-quality Ethiopian coffee from origin to cup. Our coffee is grown by smallholder farmers in traditional garden farms across Ethiopia's highlands. Every step — from sourcing green beans to roasting and packaging — is carefully handled to ensure freshness and quality.

We believe in traceability, sustainability, and honoring Ethiopian coffee heritage. Each bag tells the story of the farmers, the land, and the craft of roasting.

Our Mission

To provide customers with freshly roasted Ethiopian Arabica coffee that reflects the unique flavors of Ethiopia's highland coffee regions while supporting small-scale farmers and maintaining high quality standards.

✓ Premium Arabica beans from 1,900–2,200m altitude✓ Small-batch roasting for optimal freshness✓ Traceable sourcing from smallholder farmers✓ Premium packaging with degassing valve

Our Long-Term Vision

After the first year of operation, Bean to Bag plans to invest in dry-processing coffee land. This strategy will allow us to reduce raw coffee costs, control coffee quality, improve traceability, and increase profit margins.

Year 1: Establish brand, reach 200kg monthly sales. Year 2: Increase to 500kg/month, expand retail distribution. Year 3: Open a small retail shop, begin exporting roasted Ethiopian coffee.
