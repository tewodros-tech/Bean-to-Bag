About Bean to Bag

From origin to cup — with passion and precision

Our Story

Bean to Bag is dedicated to delivering high-quality Ethiopian coffee from origin to cup. Our coffee is grown by smallholder farmers in traditional garden farms across Ethiopia's highlands at 1,900–2,200 meters above sea level.

Every step — from sourcing green beans to roasting and packaging — is carefully handled to ensure freshness and quality. The name Bean to Bag represents the complete coffee journey: Coffee Bean → Roasting → Packaging → Customer.

We roast all our coffees to a Medium-Dark roast level, providing balanced acidity, full body, rich chocolate and cocoa notes, and a smooth finish. Our roast profile is designed to work perfectly with Jebena brewing, espresso, macchiato, French press, and milk-based drinks.

Ethiopian Highlands

Our journey begins at 1,900-2,200 meters above sea level. Small-scale farmers cultivate 100% Coffea Arabica using traditional garden farming methods, allowing cherries to ripen slowly and develop complex flavors.

Medium-Dark Roast Profile

Our signature roast brings out the best of Ethiopian Arabica — balanced acidity, full body, rich chocolate notes, and a smooth, clean finish.

Quality Control

Every batch is cupped and tested for flavor consistency. We ensure every bag meets our high standards before it reaches you.

Direct Trade

Working directly with smallholder farmers ensures fair prices and traceability from farm to cup.
