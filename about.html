Our Story

From Ethiopian highlands to your cup — with integrity, freshness, and passion.

Who We Are

Bean to Bag is dedicated to delivering high-quality Ethiopian coffee from origin to cup.

Our coffee is grown by smallholder farmers in traditional garden farms across Ethiopia's highlands. Every step — from sourcing green beans to roasting and packaging — is carefully handled to ensure freshness and quality.

We believe that great coffee tells a story. Our story begins in the ancient coffee forests of Ethiopia, where Coffea Arabica was first discovered. Today, we partner with the same farming communities to bring you coffee that honors tradition while embracing modern quality standards.

Our Core Values

Traceability

We know exactly where our coffee comes from and work directly with farmers.

Fresh Roasting

Every order is roasted in small batches and shipped within days.

Premium Packaging

Sustainable Growth

We invest back into farming communities for long-term partnerships.

Our Vision

To build a recognized Ethiopian coffee brand that delivers premium roasted coffee while maintaining full traceability from farm to final product.

Our Mission

To provide customers with freshly roasted Ethiopian Arabica coffee that reflects the unique flavors of Ethiopia's highland coffee regions while supporting small-scale farmers.
