The Sacred Coffee Ceremony

In Ethiopia, coffee is a ritual of connection. Women dressed in traditional white kemis roast green beans over open flame, pound them with a mortar, and brew three rounds — 'Abol' (first strength), 'Tona' (second), and 'Baraka' (blessing). It's a symbol of respect, friendship, and community.
