Our Story

Founded in Addis Ababa, Bean to Bag was born from a passion for sharing Ethiopia's rich coffee heritage with the world. We work directly with smallholder farmers across the Sidama, Yirgacheffe, and Guji regions to bring you the finest 100% Arabica beans.

  • Direct trade partnerships with local farmers
  • Small-batch roasting in Addis Ababa
  • Sustainable and eco-friendly practices
  • Freshness guaranteed in every bag
