Our Story

Bean to Bag is dedicated to delivering high-quality Ethiopian coffee from origin to cup.

Our Journey

2018

The vision of Bean to Bag was born - to bring authentic Ethiopian coffee to the world.

2020

Partnership with smallholder farmers in the Ethiopian highlands established.

2022

First roasting facility opened in Addis Ababa. Small-batch roasting begins.

2024

Bean to Bag launches premium packaging and expands to cafés and retailers.

2025

Growing to 200kg monthly production, serving coffee lovers worldwide.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide freshly roasted Ethiopian Arabica coffee that reflects unique highland flavors while supporting small-scale farmers.

Vision

To build a recognized Ethiopian coffee brand with full traceability from farm to final product.
