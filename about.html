Our Story

From the ancient coffee forests of Ethiopia to your cup — a journey of passion, quality, and heritage.

Bean to Bag Philosophy

Founded in the heart of Addis Ababa, Bean to Bag was born from a deep respect for Ethiopia's coffee legacy. We believe that every cup tells a story — of the highlands, the farmers, and the centuries-old traditions that make Ethiopian coffee world-renowned.

Our name reflects our commitment: we oversee every step from the green bean to the final bag. By working directly with small-scale farmers in regions like Yirgacheffe, Sidama, and Guji, we ensure fair prices, sustainable practices, and traceable quality.

Each batch is carefully roasted in our Addis Ababa facility, unlocking the unique floral, fruity, and wine-like characteristics that only Ethiopian Arabica can offer.

Our Core Values

Sustainability

We partner with eco-conscious farms that protect the land, biodiversity, and traditional cultivation methods.

Direct Trade

Eliminating middlemen ensures farmers receive fair compensation and you receive exceptional quality.

Artisanal Roasting

Small-batch roasting highlights each bean's unique profile, from bright acidity to deep chocolate notes.

Traceability

Every bag tells you the exact origin, altitude, and processing method — full transparency.
